Custom Design

Our forte and our passion. Long ago, we realized that exceeding the expectations of our clients required us to excel in the discipline of design and fabrication. So we took both activities in house, under our direct control and supervision. Our master goldsmith specializes in work with platinum and 18 karat and has 30 years of experience.

The Custom Process

A Personal Consultation

We begin by meeting you in person, getting a sense of your mission, goals, budget and style preferences. Our goal is to ask the questions that will help us achieve your vision and create an open space for education.

Selecting Materials

Once we’ve decided on the direction we are headed towards, we begin the material sourcing process. We will leverage tools and visual charts to determine the color, sizing and quality that you are looking for. We will bring a selection for you to view and give feedback on.

Creating a CAD Design

CAD designs are visual representations of the piece that are 3-D and let you see what the piece will actually look like. We will get your approval of the piece and iterate based on your feedback until we achieve your vision.

Wax Model and Initial Metal Castings

As we proceed, we will continue the iterative process with wax castings of what the piece will actually look like. In the case of rings, you will actually be able to try things on to get a sense of what matches your vision.

Final Product Approval

At the end of the process we will deliver the product to you for final approval. We will recommend insurance groups and provide an appraisal of your piece and a clear schedule for upkeep of your piece.
