Our Story

In 1987, Austin & Elkins began as a call to restore personalized service and uncompromising quality to Old Town Alexandria.

Today, we continue that tradition with over 30 years of industry expertise. We dedicate ourselves to executing your unique vision, using our pieces as a means to tell your story.

Whether it the start of a new beginning with a bespoke wedding band or the continuation of a long history with the restoration of a family heirloom, we invite you to collaborate with our founder and expert designer, Robin Austin, on a devoted and memorable shopping experience.
